

# Proposal for Enhancing Development Studies at Valley View University

This repository contains a proposal by **Solomon Yaw Adeklo** advocating for academic collaboration between the Faculty of Development Studies at Valley View University and the former Faculty of Integrated Development Studies (FIDS) at University for Development Studies Wa Campus, now University of Business and Integrated Development Studies (UBIDS).

The proposal highlights how the historical IDS programme structure at UDS during the 2008/2009 academic year was highly interdisciplinary and integrated:

* Economics & Entrepreneurship
* Environmental & Resource Studies
* Planning & Rural Development
* Communication Studies
* Governance & Political Studies
* Community-Based Field Practical Training (TTFPP)

It further encourages Valley View University to study and adopt elements of the UDS/UBIDS model in order to strengthen its Development Studies curriculum with more practical, technical, and field-based training components.
